The delivery that we know now may be a little different from how it used to be centuries ago!
Demae, wich literally means "to go infront of " is thought to have originated as early as the mid-Edo period in the 1700s. Demae was primarily reserved for the wealthy, who would send servants to let the shop shop keepers know that they wanted delivery.

One of its most popular forms became the delivery of soba noodles, an affordable dish that can be carried around without losing flavor or appearance.

Because there were no telephones or iphones, you couldnt call in as order. Deliverymen developed a skilled technique for staking towers of soba noodles and carrying them on bikes!
